## Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform

1. **Visit the application URL**: Your manager will provide the URL (e.g., `https://coaching.prefect.io`)
2. **Sign in with your work email**: Click "Sign In" and use your Prefect email address
3. **First-time setup**: You'll be prompted to create a password and verify your email
4. **Navigate the dashboard**: Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard

### Understanding Your Role

The platform has two user roles with different permissions:

**Sales Rep**:

- View your own call analyses and performance data
- Track your progress over time
- Access personalized coaching recommendations
- Search and review your past calls

**Sales Manager**:

- All rep capabilities plus:
- View all team members' data
- Compare rep performance against team averages
- Access team-wide insights and trends
- Monitor coaching effectiveness across the team

Your role is automatically assigned based on your Clerk account configuration. Contact your administrator if you believe your role is incorrect.

## For Sales Reps

### Your Dashboard

After signing in, you'll land on your personal dashboard showing:

- **Performance Summary**: Your overall coaching scores across key dimensions
- **Score Trends**: Line charts showing how your scores have changed over time
- **Skill Gaps**: Areas where you can improve most, prioritized by impact
- **Recent Wins**: Your recent successes and improvements
- **Coaching Plan**: Personalized recommendations based on your performance

#### Viewing Your Performance Trends

The dashboard shows your performance across four key dimensions:

1. **Product Knowledge**: Understanding of Prefect/Horizon features and positioning
2. **Discovery**: Ability to uncover customer needs and pain points
3. **Objection Handling**: Effectiveness in addressing customer concerns
4. **Engagement**: Building rapport and keeping prospects engaged

**Time Period Selector**:

- Click the dropdown in the top-right to change the time range
- Options: Last 7 days, 30 days, quarter, year, or all time
- Charts update automatically when you change the period

**Interpreting Scores**:

- **80-100**: Excellent performance, maintain your approach
- **60-79**: Good performance with room for improvement
- **40-59**: Needs improvement, review coaching recommendations
- **0-39**: Significant improvement needed, consider 1-on-1 coaching

### Reviewing Individual Call Analyses

To view detailed analysis of a specific call:

1. **From Dashboard**: Click on any call in your "Recent Calls" list
2. **From Search**: Use the Search page to find specific calls (see below)
3. **From Feed**: Click on call analysis items in the Insights Feed

#### Call Analysis Page

Each call analysis includes:

**Call Metadata**:

- Call title and date
- Duration
- Call type (discovery, demo, technical, negotiation)
- Product discussed (Prefect, Horizon, or both)
- Participants list with talk time breakdown

**Overall Score Badge**:

- Color-coded score (green = 80+, yellow = 60-79, red = below 60)
- Overall coaching score for the call

**Dimension Scores**:

- Individual scores for each of the four dimensions
- Click each dimension card to see detailed analysis

**Strengths**:

- What you did well on this call
- Specific examples from the transcript (highlighted in green)

**Areas for Improvement**:

- Specific areas to work on
- Examples from the transcript where you could improve (highlighted in yellow)

**Action Items**:

- Concrete next steps to improve
- Can be checked off as you complete them

**Transcript Snippets**:

- Key moments from the call
- Click timestamps to jump to that moment in Gong (if available)

**Export Options**:

- **Share**: Generate a shareable link for your manager or peers
- **Export PDF**: Download the analysis as a PDF for offline review

### Searching for Calls

Use the Search page to find specific calls:

#### Search Filters

1. **Date Range**: Select start and end dates
2. **Product**: Filter by Prefect, Horizon, or both
3. **Call Type**: Discovery, demo, technical deep dive, or negotiation
4. **Score Range**: Set minimum and maximum overall scores
5. **Objection Types**: Filter calls by objection type (pricing, timing, technical, competitor)
6. **Topics/Keywords**: Enter keywords to search for specific topics

#### Quick Filter Presets

Click preset buttons for common searches:

- **My Calls This Week**: Your calls from the last 7 days
- **High Performers**: Your calls scoring 80+
- **Needs Review**: Your calls scoring below 60
- **Discovery Calls**: All your discovery calls

#### Search Results

**View Options**:

- **Card View**: Visual cards with key information
- **Table View**: Compact table with sortable columns

**Sorting**:

- Sort by date (newest/oldest)
- Sort by score (highest/lowest)
- Sort by duration

**Pagination**:

- 20 results per page (default)
- Adjust page size: 10, 20, 50, or 100 results

#### Saving Searches

Save frequently used searches for quick access:

1. Configure your filters
2. Click "Save Search"
3. Name your search (e.g., "Low scoring discovery calls")
4. Access saved searches from the dropdown menu

#### Exporting Results

Export search results as CSV or Excel:

1. Run your search
2. Click "Export" button
3. Choose format (CSV or Excel)
4. File downloads with all visible columns

### Insights Feed

The Feed page shows chronological updates:

**Feed Item Types**:

- **Call Analyses**: New call analyses completed
- **Highlights**: Exceptional moments from your calls
- **Milestones**: Achievements and progress markers

**Filtering the Feed**:

- **Type Filter**: Show all or filter by specific item types
- **Time Filter**: Today, this week, this month, or custom range
- **New Items Badge**: Shows count of unread items

**Feed Actions**:

- **Bookmark**: Save important items for later review
- **Share**: Share highlights with your team
- **Dismiss**: Remove items from your feed

### Your Profile

Access your profile from the user menu (top-right avatar):

**Profile Settings**:

- View your name, email, and role
- Update your profile photo
- Change password
- Set notification preferences (future feature)

## For Sales Managers

Managers have access to all rep features plus additional team management capabilities.

### Team Dashboard

From your dashboard, access team data:

**Rep Selector Dropdown**:

- Click the dropdown in the top-right to view any team member's dashboard
- Select "Team Overview" to see aggregate team metrics
- Switch between reps to compare performance

### Viewing Rep Performance

When viewing a specific rep's dashboard, you'll see:

**Individual Performance**:

- Same view as reps see of their own data
- All scores, trends, and insights

**Team Comparison Overlay**:

- Gray line on charts showing team average
- Percentile indicators showing where the rep ranks
- Sample size shown for statistical context

**Coaching Notes Section** (Manager-only):

- Add private coaching notes visible only to managers
- Document 1-on-1 coaching sessions
- Track improvement plans

### Team Insights

Access team-wide insights from the Feed page:

**Team Insight Cards** (Manager-only):

- Team performance trends
- Department-wide achievements
- Comparative analysis across reps

**Types of Team Insights**:

- **Trends**: Team average scores over time
- **Comparisons**: How different reps compare on specific dimensions
- **Achievements**: Team milestones and collective improvements

### Searching All Calls

Managers can search across all team members' calls:

**Additional Search Options**:

- **Rep Filter**: Select specific rep(s) or search all
- **Multiple Reps**: Select multiple reps to compare

**Use Cases**:

- Find best-in-class examples of objection handling
- Identify common patterns across low-scoring calls
- Locate specific customer conversations for review

### Coaching Workflow Best Practices

**Weekly Review Process**:

1. **Monday Morning**: Review team insights from previous week
2. **Check Low Performers**: Identify calls scoring below 60
3. **Find Teaching Moments**: Search for high-scoring examples of skills your team needs
4. **Schedule 1-on-1s**: Use dashboard data to prioritize coaching sessions
5. **Document Progress**: Add coaching notes after each session

**Effective Coaching Sessions**:

1. **Prepare**: Review rep's dashboard and recent call analyses before meeting
2. **Share Specific Examples**: Reference actual calls and transcript snippets
3. **Compare to Best**: Show high-scoring examples from other reps (anonymously)
4. **Set Action Items**: Define concrete next steps
5. **Follow Up**: Check action item completion in next session

**Using Highlights**:

Highlights show exceptional moments across all calls:

- Share positive highlights in team meetings
- Use as training examples in onboarding
- Recognize reps publicly for great performance

## Features Overview

### Dimension Scoring Methodology

**How Scores Are Calculated**:

Each dimension is scored 0-100 based on AI analysis of the call transcript:

1. **Product Knowledge** (0-100):

   - Accuracy of product information
   - Depth of technical understanding
   - Appropriate use of features in context
   - Competitive positioning

2. **Discovery** (0-100):

   - Quality of questions asked
   - Uncovering pain points and requirements
   - Understanding customer context
   - Identifying decision-makers

3. **Objection Handling** (0-100):

   - Recognizing objections early
   - Empathy and acknowledgment
   - Providing relevant solutions
   - Moving conversation forward

4. **Engagement** (0-100):
   - Building rapport
   - Active listening signals
   - Appropriate talk ratio
   - Energy and enthusiasm

**Overall Score**:

- Weighted average of dimension scores
- Accounts for call type (e.g., discovery calls weight Discovery higher)

### Caching and Analysis Updates

**How Caching Works**:

- First analysis of a call is cached for 24 hours
- Subsequent views load instantly from cache
- "Force Reanalysis" button available if needed (e.g., after transcript correction)

**When to Force Reanalysis**:

- If you believe the transcript was updated in Gong
- If dimension scores seem incorrect
- After product knowledge updates (rare)

**Analysis Latency**:

- First analysis: 30-60 seconds (analyzing transcript)
- Cached results: Instant (<1 second)

### Data Privacy and Security

**What Data Is Stored**:

- Call metadata (title, date, participants)
- Analysis results (scores, insights, action items)
- User profiles and roles

**What Data Is NOT Stored**:

- Full call transcripts (retrieved from Gong on-demand)
- Audio or video recordings
- Customer contact information (beyond names)

**Access Control**:

- Reps can only see their own data
- Managers can see all team data
- No external sharing (all data stays within Prefect)

### Mobile Experience

The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile devices:

**Mobile Features**:

- Simplified navigation (hamburger menu)
- Touch-friendly controls
- Responsive charts and tables
- All core features available

**Best Practices on Mobile**:

- Use landscape mode for charts
- Use search filters to narrow results
- Export data for offline review on desktop

## Best Practices

### For Sales Reps

**Daily Habits**:

- Review call analyses within 24 hours of each call
- Check action items and incorporate into next calls
- Track your improvement trends weekly

**Weekly Habits**:

- Review your dashboard to spot trends
- Compare current week to previous week
- Identify one skill gap to focus on

**Monthly Habits**:

- Review your coaching plan and adjust priorities
- Celebrate improvements and wins
- Set new goals based on progress

**Making the Most of Coaching**:

- Don't take feedback personally - it's data-driven
- Focus on specific examples and action items
- Ask your manager to review specific calls with you
- Practice applying action items on your next call

### For Sales Managers

**Effective Coaching**:

- Use data to guide coaching, not replace human judgment
- Reference specific transcript examples in coaching sessions
- Celebrate improvements publicly, address gaps privately
- Track coaching effectiveness over time

**Team Development**:

- Share best practices from high-scoring calls
- Create a library of excellent examples for each dimension
- Rotate teaching moments among different reps
- Foster peer learning and coaching

**Data-Driven Decisions**:

- Use trends to inform training priorities
- Identify systemic issues vs. individual gaps
- Measure coaching impact with before/after scores
- Adjust team goals based on performance data

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How often is data updated?**
A: Call analyses are generated when you first view a call. Dashboard data updates in real-time when you refresh. Feed items appear within minutes of new analyses.

**Q: Can I delete a call analysis?**
A: No, but you can dismiss items from your feed. Contact your administrator if you need an analysis removed.

**Q: What if my role is wrong?**
A: Contact your Clerk administrator (IT team) to update your role in the system.

**Q: Can I share analyses outside the platform?**
A: You can export PDFs, but avoid sharing sensitive customer information externally. Check with your manager first.

### For Reps

**Q: Why can't I see other reps' calls?**
A: This is intentional for privacy. Only managers have cross-team visibility.

**Q: What if I disagree with a score?**
A: Discuss with your manager. You can force reanalysis if the transcript was wrong, but scores are generally consistent.

**Q: How are action items generated?**
A: AI analyzes your call and suggests specific, actionable improvements based on best practices and successful patterns.

**Q: Will this affect my performance review?**
A: Check with your manager. The tool is designed for coaching and development, not punitive evaluation.

### For Managers

**Q: Can I compare multiple reps side-by-side?**
A: Not directly in the UI currently. Export data to CSV for custom analysis.

**Q: How do I share best practices with the team?**
A: Use the Share feature on high-scoring calls or highlights. Consider creating a shared folder of excellent examples.

**Q: Can I customize dimension weights?**
A: Not currently. Weights are based on call type. Contact product team if you have specific needs.

**Q: What if I need data for a specific date range?**
A: Use the Search page with date range filters and export results.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Mobile browsers are also supported.

**Q: Why is the app slow?**
A: First analysis of a call takes 30-60 seconds. Cached results are instant. Check your internet connection if issues persist.

**Q: Can I access this offline?**
A: No, the platform requires internet connection. Export PDFs for offline review.

**Q: What if I get an error?**
A: See [TROUBLESHOOTING.md](./TROUBLESHOOTING.md) for common issues and solutions.
