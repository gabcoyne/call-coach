# Weekly Reports Guide

Understanding and using your automated weekly coaching reports.

## Overview

Every Monday at 6 AM PT, Call Coach generates a personalized weekly report based on calls from the previous week. This report is automatically sent to your email and available in the dashboard.

## Understanding Your Score

### Score Calculation

Your **average score** is calculated as:

```
Average = (Discovery + Product + Objections + Engagement) / 4
```

**Example**:

### What Scores Mean

| <60    | Poor          | Significant performance issues, immediate intervention needed |

## Acting on Your Report

### Weekly Workflow

**Monday Morning**: Receive report

- Read insights
- Note coaching priorities
- Identify high-scoring calls to learn from

**Tuesday-Thursday**: Take action

- Complete coaching activities
- Review high-scoring calls
- Practice new techniques
- Get manager feedback

**Friday**: Prepare for next week

- Note what you'll focus on
- Schedule coaching if needed
- Review practice areas
- Set goals for next week

### Using Top Calls

**Learn from Success**:

1. Open top-scoring call (Acme Corp example above)
2. Read the full analysis
3. Study what made it successful
4. Write down 3 key takeaways
5. Practice those techniques this week

**Learn from Challenges**:

1. Open low-scoring call (BuildCo example)
2. Read what could improve
3. Identify specific gap
4. Find a high-scoring call in that area
5. Compare approaches
6. Practice the better approach

### Coaching Priorities

**If High Priority Available**:

1. Schedule immediately
2. Prepare any materials
3. Complete the workshop
4. Apply learnings in next call

**If Only Medium/Optional**:

1. Schedule this week if possible
2. Or complete next week
3. Track completion for development

## FAQ

**Q: When is my report sent?**
A: Every Monday at 6 AM PT, covering the previous Monday-Sunday.

**Q: Can I get a report for a different week?**
A: You can view historical reports in the dashboard. Go to Reports â†’ Select Date Range.

**Q: Why was my score lower than I expected?**
A: Scores are based on full transcript analysis, not just outcomes. Check the evidence section to see what affected the score.

**Q: Who sees my report?**
A: Your manager can see your report. Peers don't see individual reports (only team averages).

**Q: Can I export my report?**
A: Yes - click the PDF icon to download your report.

**Q: What if I disagree with my score?**
A: Scores are Claude's assessment based on the transcript. Review the evidence, discuss with your manager, and use for learning.

**Q: How do I improve my score?**
A: 1) Identify lowest dimension 2) Study high-scoring calls 3) Practice that skill 4) Get coaching 5) Apply in real calls 6) Measure improvement next week
